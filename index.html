swoosh

the artist

the artist behind 'swoosh' is a true visionary of our time. the creator routinely manages to create thought provoking works of art by arranging simple shapes in a complex and well thought out manner. throughout his childhood he often dreamed of one day creating a magnificent interactive piece of art, a dream he later realized through 'swoosh'.

'swoosh' symbolizes the impact every member of society has on the world around them. click on the center of the interactive artwork and feel empowered by the influence you have on this piece of art.
